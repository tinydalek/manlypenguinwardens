Become a Volunteer

To become a Volunteer Penguin Warden, you must live in the Manly, Fairlight, Balgowlah area, and to be over 18years. Every volunteer must attend an information workshop before or during each penguin breeding season.


The workshop will provide information about the role and responsibilities of a NPWS volunteer as well as information about the penguin’s characteristics, breeding cycle and information regarding the relevant regulations pertinent to the Threatened Species legislation and Critical Habit regulations relating to the penguin breeding areas.


The season runs from winter to summer, so the wardens need to be able to patrol during a variety of weathers. Each volunteer is asked to give a minimum of one night per week during the breeding season, which runs from June to February to assist with protecting the penguins. A minimum of two wardens is required for each evening.


If you decide to become a volunteer penguin warden, we ask that you give an undertaking to commit for the entire breeding season to ensure that there are enough penguin wardens to cover the nightly patrols.


We are always on the lookout for Penguin Wardens. It's fun exciting and helps the environment.  For more information complete the Contact Form or email us at mec@manly.nsw.gov.au.  Alternatively, come down and visit us at the Manly Environment Centre.

Donate

The Foundation for National Parks & Wildlife has a long history of supporting the endangered colony of penguins in Manly.

Since 1999 the Foundation has been helping fund equipment for volunteer wardens, nest boxes, signage to warn people about the presence of these vulnerable birds, and much more.

Your donation will directly assist this endangered colony.

Project Penguin 

Project Penguin is a Conservation Education Program for primary school students, delivered by  Taronga Zoo’s Learning Centre in partnership with the Northern Beaches Learning Alliance.

Associated partners include Sydney Harbour NPWS Ranger, Manly Council, Manly Environment Centre, NPWS Volunteer Penguin Wardens and Manly SEALIFE Sanctuary. Find out how Project Penguin works and how you can get involved.
